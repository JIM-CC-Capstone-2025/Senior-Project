About JIM Telecommunications

Vermont's trusted partner in connectivity since 2010

Our Story

Founded in 2010, JIM Telecommunications was born from a simple mission: to bring reliable, high-speed internet to every corner of Vermont. What started as a small team serving Rutland County has grown into a comprehensive telecommunications provider serving businesses and residents throughout the state.

We understand that connectivity isn't just about speed—it's about reliability, support, and being there when our customers need us. That's why we've invested heavily in our fiber-optic network and maintain a 24/7 support team ready to assist with any issue.

Our Infrastructure

JIM Telecommunications operates a state-of-the-art fiber-optic network spanning over 2,000 miles throughout Vermont. Our redundant network architecture ensures that even if one path fails, your connection stays active through an alternate route.

We maintain three data centers strategically located across the state, each with backup power systems, climate control, and 24/7 security monitoring. This infrastructure allows us to deliver the speed and reliability that modern businesses and homes require.
